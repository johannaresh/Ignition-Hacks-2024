How it works

Webscrape, summarise , generate audiobooks and create a Braille Ready Format Doc!


Our webscraper allows you to enter a webpage and we will scrape and analyse it along with any additional documents and create a summary! This summary can further be turned into an audiobook and/or be turned into a BRF( Braille Ready Format) file!
